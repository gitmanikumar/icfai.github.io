Latin: Legum Baccalaureus

Legum Baccalaureus or LLB is a three-year Bachelor of Law degree that is offered to aspirants by many renowned colleges in India. ... The three-year LLB course offered at all law colleges of India is regulated and closely supervised by the Bar Council of India (BCI).
