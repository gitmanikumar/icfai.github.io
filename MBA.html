Master of Business Administration

MBA is a two- year post-graduate degree course in business management and administration. The course imparts knowledge and training of running a business organisation successfully. It includes key aspects such as organisational behaviour, finance management, marketing management, operations management, HR management, business communication, etc.

  2. MBA MARKETING
  3. MBA HUMAN RESOURCE MANAGEMENT (HRM)
  4. MBA INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS
  5. MBA RURAL MANAGEMENT
  6. MBA BUSINESS ANALYTICS
  7. MBA SUPPLY CHAIN
  8. MBA AGRI BUSINESS
